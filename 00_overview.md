# Jesus: From Genesis to Revelation

> *The New Testament does not begin at Matthew. It begins at Genesis.*

A promise of a seed in a garden. A ram substituted on a mountain. A lamb's blood marking doorposts during enslavement. A tabernacle establishing God's presence. A prophecy of a prophet like Moses. A king receiving an eternal throne. A suffering servant bearing sins. A son of man receiving dominion.

This Old Testament bible study traces Christ throughout Scripture, organized around seven major covenants plus the consummation — 52 weeks, 5 days per week. Each covenant section shows the Old Testament foundation, New Testament fulfillment, and a Second Coming perspective.
